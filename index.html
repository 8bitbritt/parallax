Our Moon

The Earth's moon has only been walked on by 12 American males. A person weights 16% less on the moon, which is why the astronauts were able to leap around. The moon has no atmosphere thus, the weather is always fluctuating, no sound can be heard on the moon, and the sky always appears black. The moon has quakes caused by the gravitation pull of the Earth. The moon is very slowly drifting away from the Earth at a speed of 3.8cm per year. The farther away the moon is, the longer it will take to orbit the Earth.
